About Us

klugekopf Global concept was founded by Betty Owhor in 2020 , registered under the Nigerian corporate commission, we have been operating remotely, training youths and providing job opportunities to alumni trainees.

We have successfully trained more than 10 people by providing scholarship opportunity to youths who do not possess required skills but would love to join the work force. has been operated by betty owhor and bright chukwu as a director.

Problems We Solve

Skills Gap in Emerging Technologies

The rapid evolution of technology has led to a significant skills gap, leaving individuals unprepared for roles in blockchain, Web3, and cybersecurity.

Klugekopf Global Concept bridges the skills gap by offering comprehensive training programs, empowering individuals with the knowledge and skills needed to excel in the tech industry.

Complexity in Cryptocurrency Trading

Cryptocurrency trading can be complex and daunting for newcomers, leading to potential risks and missed opportunities.

Klugekopf Global Concept simplifies cryptocurrency trading by offering insights, market analysis, and strategic guidance, enabling clients to make informed and profitable trading decisions and to be able to buy and sell cryptocurrency with ease.

Limited Diversity in Tech

The tech industry often lacks diversity, with underrepresented groups facing barriers to entry.

We promote diversity and inclusivity through our educational programs, ensuring that opportunities in tech are accessible to individuals from all backgrounds.
